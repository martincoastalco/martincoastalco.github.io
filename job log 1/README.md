# Field Log — GitHub Pages Setup

## Upload these 3 files to GitHub:
- index.html
- manifest.json
- icon.png

## Steps:

1. Go to github.com and sign in
2. Click the green "New" button to create a repository
3. Name it: field-log
4. Make sure it is set to Public
5. Click "Create repository"
6. Click "uploading an existing file"
7. Drag all 3 files into the upload area
8. Click "Commit changes"
9. Go to Settings (top tab of your repo)
10. Scroll down to "Pages" in the left sidebar
11. Under "Branch" select "main" and click Save
12. Wait about 60 seconds, then your app is live at:
    https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io/field-log

## Install on iPhone:
1. Open that URL in Safari (not Chrome)
2. Tap the Share button at the bottom (box with arrow pointing up)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"
5. The app icon appears on your home screen

## Your data:
- Saves automatically to your phone — never disappears
- Use Backup button inside the app to email yourself a backup
